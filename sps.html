The Society of Physics Students

The Society of Physics Students (SPS) is a professional association designed for undergraduate students across the United States. I joind SPS shortly after joining the Physics department at ETSU, and immedietly felt at home. I have since grown within the association to hold titles such as ETSU Chapter President, Associate Zone Counselor (AZC) for Zone 8, and the AZC Representative. As AZC Rep, I am the sole voice for all students nationwide on the Executive Committee, the highest position an undergraduate can achieve within the organization. Before my election as AZC, I planned and hosted a regional conference for Zone 8 at ETSU. I also established a chapter of Sigma Pi Sigma, the national Physics & Astronomy Honor Society, and planned our inaugural induction ceremony. These experiences have sculpted my leadership skills and administrative capability.

Faith Simmons | Physics & Astronomy
