About Me

I graduated from East Tennessee State University with a Bachelor of Science in Physics. As a McNair Scholar, I spent a year conducting astronomy research focused on the dynamics of massive stars and stellar evolution. Alongside my research, I serve as the Associate Zone Counselor Representative on the Executive Committee of the Society of Physics Students. I am also deeply engaged in scientific outreach and communication, with a strong interest in making physics and astronomy accessible to broader audiences. Outside of academia, I love performing aerial arts and racing dirt bikes. I also have an amazing husband, Evan, and pup, Loki! You can check out some photos here.
